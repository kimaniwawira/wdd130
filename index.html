Nairobi

Nairobi is the capital city and the largest city of Kenya. It lies on the South-central part of Kenya. It has a varied topography showcasing modern skyscrapers, colonial-era architecture, and lush green spaces that has it dubbed 'the green city under the sun'. It is the home to the newly dedicated Nairobi Kenya Temple. The only city that hosts a National park within it.
